Account Deletion Request

Noteva

Last updated: February 12, 2026

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you may request deletion using the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

To request account deletion, please send an email to:

heaticdeveloper@gmail.com

Include the following information:

  • Your account UID (found in app settings)
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
What Happens After Request
  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • All notes and stored data will be removed
  • This action cannot be reversed
Processing Time

Requests are processed within 7 business days.

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data after deletion is completed.

Need Help?

If you experience issues requesting deletion, contact us at the email above and we will assist you.
